About us

The Best Family legacy is built around sharing in a vision to deliver your dream concepts, into reality. And the only way we believe that should be done, is through the highest level of service throughout the entire project, including project management, support staff and skilled on-site labour The only thing that matters to the Best Builder team – is that your family is happy in your new/refreshed home. Company Overview For over 15 years, the Best Builder team has been offering clients a complete professional homebuilding package thanks to extensive knowledge and experience in design, contract procedures, project management and trade skills. Best Builders employs skilled in-house carpenters and experienced administrative support to ensure project deadlines and budget goals are respected.  Approaching new home construction or a renovation can be overwhelming at times; we believe in educating clients throughout the entire process so they can enjoy the experience of seeing their ideas and dreams becoming reality. .

Custom homes

Let us be part of the process from the initial design stage. This ensures that we meet all of your budget and design objectives. Having the builder involved from the very beginning is definitely the best way to go about designing and building your new dream home or renovation!
We can help you from your initial lot selection and acquisition, through the design stage, demolition of existing structure, construction of your new custom home or cottage, as well as completing the entire landscape package. We also provide all interior designs, exterior designs and product selections for you.
We can provide all or some of the services mentioned above depending on your scenario and wishes. We offer several levels of construction and finishing details.This way we can fine tune the budget to meet your needs. We also offer a complete secure virtual cloud management system that allows you access to scheduling, documents, project pictures 24/7 messaging and communication, as well as change order approvals and product selections.
